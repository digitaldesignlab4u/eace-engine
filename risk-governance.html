What's included

Three components.
One governance system.

XLSX

Excel AIMS Engine

27-sheet workbook structured to ISO/IEC 42001 AIMS. Risk scoring, control mapping, lifecycle gates, supplier risk, HITL logs, FRIA/DPIA integration, and compliance dashboard. Upload XLSX to the HTML dashboard for visual reporting.

HTML

Offline Dashboard

Static dashboard that reads your XLSX data locally. Risk by domain chart, EU AI Act obligations tracker, top risks table, compliance score. Upload & Export PDF — no server, no API, no cloud connection required.

DOCX

Documentation Package

Evidence record templates, risk assessment documentation, governance procedures, and a structured evidence pack for audit submission. Aligned with ISO 42001 section structure.

Key capabilities

Operational governance.
From day one.

—Risk scoring across 8 domains: AI & Model Risk · Cyber/Security · Data & Privacy · Compliance/Legal · BCM/Operational · Finance · HR · Security
—Risk-control mapping with likelihood, impact, and residual score calculation
—EU AI Act key obligations tracker (AIA-001 through AIA-006 and beyond)
—Lifecycle gates — from design through deployment, monitoring, and decommissioning
—FRIA (Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment) integration — Art. 27
—DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment) integration
—HITL (Human-in-the-Loop) logging — Art. 14 mandatory for high-risk AI
—AI supplier and third-party risk assessment
—AI Model Inventory and EU AI Act Compliance register
—Control Library with owner assignment and status tracking
—AI Incident Register with severity classification
—PDF export from HTML dashboard for board reporting and audit submission
